Dal

Description

This is a recipe for dal, a lentil dish from South Asia. It is simple to make yet quite flavorful.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups split red lentils
  • Half a sliced onion
  • As much chopped garlic as you would like
  • 2 cups water
  • Spices

Steps

  1. Soak 2 cups of lentils in 2 cups of water overnight
  2. Chop half an onion
  3. Chop up some garlic
  4. Sautee onions and garlic
  5. Add soaked lentils and water to pot
  6. Add spices
  7. Simmer until soft
  8. Enjoy!
